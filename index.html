About Us

Hey there! I'm a linguist and polyglot who spends a lot of their time learning languages. A lot of this time is spent learning new alphabets, and I wanted a tool that would rapid memorize new characters with their English equivalents for free. After building this, I decided to also add an option to practice with full words, so you can see the characters in context with other characters. I hope you enjoy the app!
